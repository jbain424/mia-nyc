Our Mission

Made In Africa NYC is an entertainment company that is dedicated to promoting African culture through social gatherings, concerts, community services, and social media platforms. The company was founded in 2011 with the sole purpose to bridge the gap between the diaspora and the world by developing and creating unique cultural experiences that engages, uplifts, and retains diverse members of the diaspora in a professional and tres chic social setting.
